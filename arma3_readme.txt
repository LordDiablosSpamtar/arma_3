Installation
============

System Requirements (minimum):
------------------------------
• OS: Windows Vista SP2 / Windows 7 SP1
• CPU: Intel Dual-Core 2.4 GHz / AMD Dual-Core Athlon 2.5 GHz
• GPU: NVIDIA GeForce 8800GT / AMD Radeon HD 3830 / Intel HD Graphics 4000 with 512 MB VRAM
• DirectX®: 10
• RAM: 2 GB
• HDD: 15 GB free space
• Audio: DirectX®-compatible on-board
• Other:
         Internet connection and free Steam account to activate
         Dual Layer compatible when installing from DVD

System Requirements (recommended):
---------------------------------
• OS: Windows Vista SP2 / Windows 7 SP1
• CPU: Intel Core i5-2300 / AMD Phenom II X4 940
• GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 560 / AMD Radeon HD 7750 with 1 GB VRAM
• DirectX®: 11
• RAM: 4 GB
• HDD: 25 GB free space, SSD / Hybrid HDD / SSHD storage
• Audio: DirectX®-compatible soundcard
• Other:
         Internet connection and free Steam account to activate
         Dual Layer compatible when installing from DVD

Setup
-----
When installing Arma 3 from a purchased DVD, place the disc in your PC's disc tray. The Setup application should start. Alternatively, you can start Setup.exe in your DVD drive's root folder manually. Please select to install the game from the Setup menu and follow the instructions provided in the process.

When installing Arma 3 from Steam, please follow the instructions provided in the Steam client application.

DirectX
-------
If you encounter a problem installing DirectX during the installation, please update DirectX manually. DirectX June 2010 is required in order to run the game, you can find it at http://www.microsoft.com/directx


Overview
========
Experience true combat gameplay in a massive military sandbox. Authentic, diverse, open - Arma 3™ sends you to war.

Key features
------------
• Altis & Stratis - Defeat your enemy on richly detailed battlefields – stretching over 290 km2 of Mediterranean island terrain.
• Solo & Multiplayer - Experience military gameplay by completing the singleplayer showcase missions. Run through the competitive firing drills to hone your shooting and movement skills. Form a squad and team up to fight against your enemy in multiplayer scenarios.
• Weapons & Vehicles - Master air, land, and sea with 20+ vehicles; choose your gear, pick from 40+ weapons, and customize your loadout.
• Content Creation - Start creating your own experiences with the powerful scenario editor; develop mods for others to play; share and discover scenarios on Steam Workshop; join our creative community.
• Revamped Engine - Navigate the battlefield with fluid new animations; feel the devastating power of combat with the upgraded sound engine, new ragdoll simulation and PhysX™-supported vehicles.
• Campaign Inbound - Supplied as free DLC after release, the official campaign will be deployed in 3 gameplay-driven episodes: Survive, Adapt, Win.

----------------------
• In order to verify the data you have downloaded was not corrupted during transfer, please use the Steam data cache verification option.
• Make sure to do a clean installation of the Arma 3 (uninstall first - by removing local game cache).
• Make sure all the necessary drivers are up-to-date.
